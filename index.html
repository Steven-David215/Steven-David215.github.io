You can call me Steve

Hello traveller, my name is Steven David, I go by SteveDave online and you can call me Steve. I’m a Mohawk of Akwesasne studying Game Programming at St. Lawrence College in Cornwall, Ontario set to graduate in Spring 2022.
I have previously completed St. Lawrence College’s Automotive Service Technician Diploma and worked in the Automotive field for 5 years in Peterborough, Ontario while supporting my partner as she attended Trent University. While in Peterborough I had participated in the Dragon Boat festival with Holiday Ford and volunteered as Trent Archery Club’s Instructor of Intermediate Archers, certified by the Coaching Association of Canada.
My goals moving forward after my current studies are to pursue a Bachelors in a Computer Science field, ideally of a Game/Level Design stream and to make and release projects while continuing my studies.
I’m a product of the 90’s, a fan of: Anime, Nintendo, RPG’s, fantasy stories and lore. I have a history of attending Anime Conventions to meet other like-minded fans, play video games and attend informational panels.
